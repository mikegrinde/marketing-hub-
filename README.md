# Marketing Hub — AI-Powered Campaign Planning
A campaign coordination tool built for Product and Lifecycle marketing teams.
## The Problem
Running a campaign requires three things that are almost never connected:
- **Who to target** — audience data sits in a CDP but translating it into a usable segment requires manual work

- **What to say** — messaging is disconnected from the audience and campaign goal

- **Campaign planning** — no single place to define parameters and see everything in one view
## What It Does
Marketing Hub ties all three together:
1. Define a campaign — goal, product, tactic, and channels

2. Automatically query the CDP and surface a matched audience with segment breakdowns

3. Generate creative recommendations — narratives, subject lines, and asset suggestions tailored to the campaign configuration
## Who It's For
Product and Lifecycle marketers who want to go from "I need a campaign" to "here's who to reach and what to say" — without jumping between four different tools.
